Dr. Lefavour 
 
 
Nancy G. Lefavour, DVM grew up in central New Jersey in a pet-loving family. She was surrounded by many types of pets from a young age, and took riding lessons as a teenager. She graduated from Cornell University in 2002 where she majored in Animal Science, then spent two years working in Massachusetts on an educational farm and at a small animal veterinary clinic. She then attended Tufts University School of Veterinary Medicine and earned her Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine in 2008.
